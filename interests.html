Entertainment

National Football League

American football is one of my favorite sports to watch. Let me tell you a story of mine when I first got here in United States. At first, I didn’t know what American football was, until one day all my family gathered up in our place to watch it. It was Sunday night when Seattle Seahawks team played against San Francisco 49ers. My family was all hyped up during the game. They were cheering and shouting at the TV. I felt the excitement and started liking it more whenever a team makes a touchdown. I never realized how football has helped me relieve some stress and how Sundays became our family gathering day.

National Basketball Association

Another sport I like doing is basketball. Playing basketball is another way to stay fit and healthy. It can also help you to make new friends who are young or old. Sometimes, it is not only enjoyable, but it also teaches you to improve yourself and how to be a good team player. In fact, basketball is known as a good pastime in many people’s eyes, but it can also be seen as a passion for many others. I love to watch and play the game because it is a very entertaining sport, and it can help me to release stress. This is also another way to stay connected to our society since this sport is popular and can be played by anyone.

Anime

There are many reasons why Anime has taken my interest. If I would have to explain it, it would be the life lessons behind all the movies and series of each Anime I have watched. Anime is filled with attractive lessons and life stories. People mistake it as childish, and unrealistic just because it has animation, but in many ways, it surpasses many live action series in terms of unrealistic. It is deep and very emotional when it comes to connection. It provides powerful themes, cohesive plots, and a vast variety of different topics to capitalize on. So, whenever I have free time, I spend most of my time watching Anime. It has become part of me now.
